Welcome to Tanvir Law

Tanvir Ahmed has been a corporate lawyer for 11 years in a large Public Sector Finance Corporation called National Development Finance Corporation.


He has been an Immigration consultant and an accredited interpreter with the Immigration and Refugee Board as well as with the Ministry of the Attorney General for two decades.


As a result of his extensive involvement with Immigration, Criminal and Family law, he decided to focus his Law practice on these areas in order to serve in the best way possible.


In his role as a Family Lawyer, his priority is to protect families from disintegration by doing all that is possible within the scope of the law. In his role as a Criminal Defence Lawyer, he is committed to providing competent, quality Criminal defense for a fair and reasonable fee.


As an Immigration Lawyer, he will efficiently aid clients with all matters related to immigration, work, education, unification of family, and obtaining refugee status.
